About Maruthi Tech

Excellence in fabrication since 2009

Maruthi Tech is a leading manufacturer specializing in the fabrication of high-quality stainless steel and mild steel products. Our expertise lies in preparing, cutting, shaping, and welding materials to meet the exact specifications of our customers.

With a commitment to precision and efficiency, we provide customized solutions, including the erection and installation of machines at customer sites. At Maruthi Tech, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional craftsmanship and innovative fabrication services, ensuring customer satisfaction and cost efficiency.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, customized fabrication solutions with efficiency and precision, meeting the exact needs of our customers.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative and reliable fabrication services, recognised for our excellence in craftsmanship and customer satisfaction.

Our Services

Comprehensive fabrication solutions tailored to your needs

Custom Fabrication

Preparation, cutting, shaping, and welding using advanced welding machines and power tools to fabricate products as per customer specifications.

Machine Erection

Professional on-site erection and installation of machines at the customer's location with precision and efficiency.

Manufacturing

All kinds of Stainless Steel (SS), Mild Steel (MS) & Aluminium Fabrications. Specializing in SS RO skid, filter tanks, and custom housing solutions.
